About

The Curator

Shreyash Srivastva is a digital archivist and curator specializing in the preservation and presentation of historical scientific illustrations. With a background in both art history and information science, Shreyash bridges the gap between historical scholarship and contemporary digital presentation.

This collection represents years of research across libraries, museums, and archives worldwide, bringing together rare diagrams, charts, and illustrations that chronicle humanity's evolving understanding of the natural world.

The Vision

The Shape of Knowledge explores how visual representation has shaped scientific thought across centuries. From early astronomical diagrams to intricate color theory wheels, each piece reveals the intimate relationship between seeing and understanding.

These artifacts remind us that science is not merely data, but a profoundly human endeavor to make sense of our world through observation, creativity, and visual imagination.

Mission Statement

"To preserve and celebrate the visual language of scientific discovery, making accessible the beautiful and often overlooked illustrations that transformed our understanding of reality itself."
