Description

Part Weird, Part Fascinating by Shreyash Srivastva is a poetry collection that explores raw emotion, surreal reflection, and the contradictions of everyday existence. Through free form and experimental verse, the work captures love, heartbreak, longing, and existential doubt with striking honesty. Love tastes like glucose, heartbreak vibrates like static, and identity unfolds under neon tinted thoughts. The poems move between confession and quiet rebellion, blending vulnerability with wit. It is a collection for readers who feel deeply and are drawn to beauty within strangeness.

Theme

In this work, heavy emotions are balanced with humor and playful imagery, while surreal observations reveal deeper truths about identity and perception. By rejecting rigid structure, the poetry creates space for authenticity and reflection. It invites readers to embrace the unusual, to question gently, and to find connection in the peculiar textures of human experience.
